Tacos

Description

Mexican birria tacos, Jalisco-style, made with braised beef that's slow-cooked in a fragrant 3-chile sauce with a delicious spice mix. Crispy tacos, and tender, mouth-watering beef team up with melted Mexican cheese in this impressive meal. This recipe takes a little time but it's so worth it!

Ingredients

  • Dried chile peppers
  • Beef
  • Seasoning
  • Olive oil
  • Tomatoes
  • Vinegar
  • Tortillas
  • Onion and Cilantro

Steps

  1. Boil Dried Peppers:Boil the dried peppers for 5 minutes, then remove from heat and let the peppers soak until the water is cool. Drain, reserving some water.
  2. Cook the Meat: Season the beef and sear on all sides in oil in a Dutch oven.
  3. Grill Tomatoes Grill the tomatoes until the skin is blistered and begins to peel.
  4. Blend Chili Peppers Blend the soaked chili peppers with the reserved chile water, tomatoes, vinegar, and seasonings.
  5. Strain Sauce Strain the sauce and pour over the meat. Cover the Dutch oven and cook until the meat begins to fall apart, basting often. Allow the meat to rest, then shred.
  6. Warm Torillas Warm the tortillas on the griddle. Fill each tortilla with meat, onions, and cilantro.
